1. Project Title

UniQuiz AI: Personalized Exam Generator for Higher Education 

2. Selected SDG & Justification

SDG 4: Quality Education.
+2


Justification: Our project aligns with SDG 4 by improving learning access and skill development through technology. Currently, students face a gap between general study materials and the specific requirements of their university's curriculum. UniQuiz AI bridges this gap by providing high-quality, institution-specific practice resources, ensuring inclusive and equitable quality education for all university students.
+1

3. Problem Statement
Many university students struggle to find practice exams or quizzes that accurately reflect their specific university's syllabus and professional standards. This leads to inefficient study sessions and a lack of preparedness for actual assessments.

4. Proposed Solution
UniQuiz AI is an agile software solution that utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate tailored practice quizzes. By inputting their specific university, major, and topic, students receive custom-generated Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs) and detailed explanations, mimicking the standard of their own academic institution.
+1

5. Target Audience
University students globally seeking targeted exam preparation.

Lifelong learners in specialized professional fields.

6. Core Features (Product Backlog Highlights)
Institution-Specific Generation: Selection of specific universities and majors.

AI-Powered Explanations: Instant feedback and logical reasoning for every answer.

Difficulty Scaling: Adjustable quiz levels from 'Basic' to 'Exam-ready'.
